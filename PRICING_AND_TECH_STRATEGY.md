# GetDeaddicted Academy - Pricing & Technology Strategy

## Voice AI: ElevenLabs (Recommended)

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Flash (pay-as-you-go) | ~$30 one-time | All 415 module narrations |
| Pro (1 month) | $99 one-time | Premium voices + commercial rights |

- Pre-render all audio as MP3s, serve as static files = zero ongoing cost
- 415 modules x ~200 words = ~498,000 characters total
- Hume AI better for interactive chatbots, not pre-recorded narration

## Avatar with Lip Sync: HeyGen Creator (Recommended)

| Option | Cost | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| HeyGen Creator | $29/mo (2-3 months) | Unlimited videos, 700+ avatars, 1080p |
| SadTalker (open source) | ~$50 GPU rental | Free software, needs technical setup |
| D-ID Advanced | $299/mo | 65 min/mo, expensive for volume |
| Synthesia Creator | $69/mo | 360 min/year, good quality |

- 830 minutes of video total across all modules
- Pre-render all videos, serve as static MP4s
